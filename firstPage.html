Welcome to ESTUDIAR

Welcome to ESDUDIAR! An entry level Spanish learning game. Spanish is a language used by hundreds of millions across the whole world! Pick up a couple of words or phrases and you'll be one step closer to mastering the Spanish language.


You have flash cards of a set of entry level Spanish words and phrases that you will iterate through. The flip button lets you see what the word or phrase is in Spanish. It will be a self scoring game so don't worry about getting any wrong, it is only there to let you know what you know. When you are done going through the set, just reset and get some more practice.


After you get all the words correct the game will detect that you are finished and reset the game so you can get some more reps in!
